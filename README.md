# 📊 Online Food Sales Analysis & Product Strategy

## 🌟 Overview
This project focuses on analyzing online food sales data to identify key business insights and formulate actionable product strategies. By analyzing over **3,000 transactions**, this project aims to optimize revenue, improve customer retention, and refine distribution strategies across various digital channels.

---

## 🚀 Key Features
*   **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** Deep understanding of monthly sales trends, top-selling products, and customer demographic profiles.
*   **Daily Demand Forecasting:** Implementation of **Random Forest** and **Linear Regression** models to predict daily demand with high accuracy.
*   **Interactive Dashboard:** Comprehensive data visualization using **Power BI** for real-time KPI monitoring.
*   **Strategic Recommendations:** Data-driven strategies for customer acquisition and profit margin optimization through channel management.

## 📈 Key Insights & Product Strategy
Based on the data analysis, here are the main findings and strategic recommendations:

### 1. Customer Ecosystem Balance
*   **Insight:** The proportion of new customers (1,506) and returning customers (1,494) is almost balanced (50:50). This indicates strong acquisition appeal and healthy retention rates.
*   **Strategy:** Implement a point-based loyalty program for returning customers and "welcome incentives" to convert new customers into regulars.

### 2. Sales Channel Optimization
*   **Insight:** Revenue is evenly distributed across three main channels: **Third-Party App** (Rp108.3 Million), **Mobile App** (Rp106 Million), and **Website** (Rp104.3 Million).
*   **Strategy:** Execute a "soft migration" from Third-Party Apps to the brand's own Mobile App through exclusive promotions to reduce high commission fees and increase net profit margins.

### 3. Product & Time Trends
*   **Insight:** **Boba Milk Tea** is the top-selling product. Sales peak during evening hours, especially on weekends.
*   **Strategy:** Deployment of "Evening Deals" during peak hours and increasing inventory for high-demand products like Boba Milk Tea ahead of peak periods.
